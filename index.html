UNTIL THE DIE

After the tragic death of her parents, Felicia, a 18-year-old teenager, sinks into a murderous rage, becoming a cold and ruthless killer to avenge their death. She struggles to escape from the police and confront the demons that torment her. On top of all that, Felicia finds herself torn between two impossible loves: Andrew, the leader of the Italian mafia, who embodies the devil himself, and Jason, the handsome police officer who swears by justice like an angel. Felicia will have to make a crucial choice: will she let Andrew fulfill her dream of revenge or will she allow Jason to save her soul? This captivating novel will plunge you into Felicia's complex and dark world, where vengeance and madness blend to create a thrilling and emotional story.
